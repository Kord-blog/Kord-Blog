### The Problem
Most solo-preneurs waste 60 minutes manually setting up folders and sending "Welcome" emails every time they get a new client. That is an hour of "Non-Billable Friction" (work you don't get paid for).

### The Systemic Fix
1. **The Intake:** Use **Tally.so** (it's free) to collect client info.
2. **The Sorting:** Set a **Gmail Filter** to automatically label these emails as "URGENT."
3. **The Kickoff:** Use a **Text Expander** (like the free version of Magical) to send your "Welcome Packet" in two keystrokes.

**Result:** You save 55 minutes per client. Total cost: $0.
